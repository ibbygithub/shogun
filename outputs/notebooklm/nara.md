## Sightseeing

### Kasugataisha Shrine / 春日大社
📍 160 Kasuganocho, Nara City, Nara 630-8212
🏷️ group, sightseeing, early-morning
👥 Crowd: Far fewer tourists than Todai-ji despite being equally significant. 10 min walk from the main deer area. The approach path lined with 3,000 stone lanterns is stunning.
⏰ Best time: Open 6am. Morning is best — lantern-lined approach through forest is atmospheric. If the lantern festival dates coincide (~Feb/Aug) it is extraordinary.

### Naramachi Historic District / ならまち
📍 Naramachi, Nara City, Nara
🏷️ group, sightseeing, early-morning
👥 Crowd: Almost tourist-free before 10am. Preserved Edo-period merchant townhouses (machiya). Boutique cafes and craft shops.
⏰ Best time: Before 10am for the streets. Most cafes open 10-11am — circle back after the deer park for a late-morning coffee and browse.

### Nara Park (Deer Park) / 奈良公園
📍 Zoshicho, Nara City, Nara 630-8212
🏷️ group, sightseeing, early-morning, crowd-warning, cherry-blossom
👥 Crowd: Tour buses arrive 9am-10am in waves. After 10am it is very crowded during cherry blossom peak. ~1,200 wild deer roam freely — they bow for shika senbei (deer crackers) sold at kiosks.
⏰ Best time: ARRIVE BEFORE 9AM. Late March = cherry blossom peak — early morning light through the blossoms with deer is exceptional. Buy deer crackers at kiosks (150 yen). Hold bag away from your body.

### Todai-ji Temple (Great Buddha) / 東大寺（大仏）
📍 406-1 Zoshicho, Nara City, Nara
🏷️ group, sightseeing, early-morning, crowd-warning
👥 Crowd: Opens 7:30am. World's largest wooden building. Most tour groups arrive after 10am. Entry fee ~600 yen.
⏰ Best time: 7:30-9am. The Great Buddha (15m bronze Vairocana) is genuinely awe-inspiring. Also look for the pillar with the nostril-sized hole — fitting through it is said to grant enlightenment.
